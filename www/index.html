How Trojan Points Work

  • You may give out a maximum of 35 points per week
  • For every one point awarded the sender will receive ½ point.
  • Points will be automatically replenished each week.
  • Remaining points do not rollover to the next week.
  • Derogatory comments and/or profanity will NOT be accepted and will be immediately removed.
  • Further disciplinary action may take place based on severity.
  • The news feed will be monitored by an administrator on a regular basis.
  • Abuse or unfair point distribution will NOT be accepted.
  • Once a month a gift card raffle will take place. For every 25 submissions that month, one $25.00 gift card will be awarded to a Trojan Point recipient.
  • If a department reaches 25 submissions, one of the gift cards will be awarded to a Trojan Point recipient from that department.
  • All winners will be chosen by raffle.
